This tool helps to map your whole career journey so far, across all companies you have worked for, not just COTY.

The three rings of the wheel refer to the three stages of your lifetime career: Early (In your first 10 years), Mid (In your career years of 11-20) and Later (after the 20th year of career experience).

Once you start, type in your name and click start. Please note once started do not refresh browser, or leave open and return later, as progress will not be saved.

The wheel is composed of 6 domains in the center and each domain consists of different areas of experiences, at the outside of the Wheel. To see what each area of experiences means you can hoover over these words and see the definitions.

Click on a segment you wish to complete within the wheel. A box will pop-up to enter the Job Title and Company where you gained this experience. Input them and then click ADD. Each time you do this the relevant segment of the wheel will become colored-in and the role information will be added below the wheel in the correct color code. You can use the same role to fill in multiple areas of experience if appropriate.

You can delete your role details at any time, just press X on the box below the wheel of the role you would like to delete.

Once happy with all your input you can click PRINT and this will open your print window. You need to make sure that you select option "Background Graphics" and click select save as PDF or Print
