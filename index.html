This tool helps to map your whole career journey so far, across all companies you have worked for, not just COTY.

The three rings of the wheel refer to the three stages of your lifetime career: Early (In your first 10 years), Mid (In your career years of 11-20) and Later (after the 20th year of career experience).

Once you start, type in your name and click start. Please note once started do not refresh browser, or leave open and return later, as progress will not be saved.

The wheel is composed of different domains in the center and each domain consists of different areas of experiences, at the outside of the wheel.

Click on a segment you wish to color within the wheel. A box will pop-up to enter the Job Title and Company where you gained this experience. Input them and then click ADD. Each time you do this the relevant segment of the wheel will become colored-in and the segment information will be added below the wheel in the correct color code. You can use the same segment to fill in multiple areas of experience if appropriate.

You can delete your segment details at any time, just press X on the box below the wheel of the segment you would like to delete. You will be asked if you want this section on the Wheel cleared. Clicking OK will clear all color from this section of the wheel.

Once happy with all your input you can click PRINT and this will open your print window. You need to make sure that you select option "Background Graphics" and click select save as PDF or Print.
